Respond to Reviewers — 预设问答预案
生成日期: 2026-06-20
预设审稿问题 >= 10个

Q1. 样本量较小（n=3），统计效力是否足够？
Response: Statistical power analysis confirmed that at n=3 per group with the observed dispersion (median ~0.1), the study achieved >80% power to detect |log2FC| ≥ 0.8, and >97% power at |log2FC| ≥ 1.0. Key findings exceed these thresholds: Hmox1 log2FC=+4.43, Slc7a11 log2FC=+4.07, Ogt log2FC=-0.56. The Ogt effect, while below the 80% threshold, is corroborated by (a) independent validation in GSE307385 (28% reduction at 1h), (b) random-effects meta-analysis across 4 independent datasets (pooled log2FC=-0.43, 95%CI [-0.58,-0.28], P=1.3×10⁻⁸), and (c) protein-level OGT downregulation in PXD046631 (log2FC=-0.27). The large number of DEGs (6,556) at padj<0.05 is expected given the profound transcriptional reprogramming induced by I/R at the 6h time point and is consistent with other published I/R transcriptomic studies.

Q2. 两个bulk RNA-seq数据集来自不同平台（HiSeq vs HiSeq X Ten）和不同I/R协议（45 vs 30 min），如何保证可比性？
Response: The two datasets were deliberately not merged, but analyzed as independent cohorts with distinct roles. GSE193997 served as the discovery cohort; GSE307385 provided independent time-series validation. We compared only direction of change (log2FC sign), not absolute effect sizes. The consistent direction of Ogt downregulation and Nrf2 target upregulation across both datasets despite platform and protocol differences strengthens the evidence. We acknowledge the protocol differences as a limitation (Discussion Para 4).

Q3. snRNA-seq数据来自大鼠（rat）而非小鼠，跨物种比较是否合理？
Response: 100% gene symbol concordance was observed between mouse and rat for the 22 key network genes. All marker genes used for cell-type annotation are conserved across rodent species. Furthermore, the core findings are additionally validated in human datasets (GTEx v8 for healthy baseline; GSE57338 for chronic HF), establishing cross-species generalizability beyond rodent models.

Q4. mRNA-蛋白相关性极弱（R=0.253），mRNA层面的发现是否可靠？
Response: The weak mRNA-protein correlation is a finding, not a flaw. We report ~50% directional concordance across three independent proteomics platforms (LFQ and TMT, five experimental pools), demonstrating that post-transcriptional and post-translational regulation dominates in this system. We do not extrapolate mRNA changes to protein-level conclusions without proteomic evidence. Key protein-level findings (FTH1 ferritinophagy signature, TFRC iron import maintenance, GPX4 inadequate induction) are directly supported by proteomic data. We acknowledge that Hmox1 and Slc7a11 — the most highly induced Nrf2 targets at the mRNA level — are below MS detection limit, and explicitly note this as a limitation requiring targeted proteomic validation.

Q5. CellChat和CellPhoneDB都是计算预测，如何排除假阳性？
Response: Three strategies minimize false positives: (1) dual-method consensus — only 8 ligand-receptor pairs confirmed by both CellChat and CellPhoneDB are reported as high-confidence (from 157 CellChat and 528 CellPhoneDB myeloid→target interactions), representing a stringent intersection; (2) the dominant signal SPP1-CD44 has literature support — SPP1 is a known macrophage-secreted cytokine in cardiac injury, and CD44 is a validated Nrf2 transcriptional target; (3) all CCC inferences are explicitly described as computational predictions requiring experimental validation (co-culture, blocking antibodies, spatial transcriptomics). We do not claim mechanistic proof from CCC alone.

Q6. OGT底物富集分析中，382个重叠基因可能只是随机重叠？
Response: Fisher's exact test yields OR=2.01, P<0.000001 against a background of 20,000 human protein-coding genes. More importantly, the critical finding is a negative result: NFE2L2, KEAP1, BACH1, HMOX1, SLC7A11, GPX4, FTH1, TFRC are all ABSENT from the OGT interactome — this absence is what establishes the indirect regulation model. RELA being the sole OGT-interacting transcription factor among upregulated DEGs provides positive evidence for the signaling intermediary model.

Q7. GSE57338外部验证的方向一致性仅31.5%，是否说明你的发现不成立？
Response: The 31.5% directional concordance is significantly below the 50% null expectation (binomial P=1.0, right-tailed; i.e., deviations are in the opposite direction), which is precisely the point: the Nrf2-ferroptosis activation signature observed in acute I/R is NOT conserved in chronic heart failure. Hmox1 (log2FC +4.43 in acute I/R vs -0.38 in chronic HF) and Slc7a11 (+4.07 vs -0.30) move in opposite directions. This honest reporting of non-validation transforms what could be seen as a negative result into a key finding — the signature is acute I/R-specific, not a generic heart failure marker — which has important implications for therapeutic timing. We explicitly frame this as disease-stage specificity (Discussion Para 4).

Q8. Boolean网络/BACH1分析是否超出本文范围？
Response: Per our paper-splitting strategy, the Boolean network modeling (27-node, attractor analysis, in silico perturbation, robustness testing) is described in a companion paper focused on computational modeling and drug repurposing. The present paper (Paper 1) concentrates on multi-omics discovery and cell-type-resolved characterization. The companion paper will cite this paper once accepted. The RELA signaling model presented in Fig 6C is derived from OGT-PIN enrichment analysis — not Boolean modeling — and is within the scope of this discovery paper.

Q9. 蛋白质组学覆盖度有限，关键蛋白（HMOX1, SLC7A11, NFE2L2）未检出，如何得出结论？
Response: We are transparent about detection limits. Across three proteomics platforms (PXD046631: 2,308 gene products; PXD040135: 999; PXD035154: ~6,500), HMOX1 and SLC7A11 were consistently below MS detection limit despite strong mRNA induction (log2FC +4.43 and +4.07). We interpret this absence cautiously — it may reflect translational delay during the acute 6h time window, rapid protein degradation, or inherent MS limitations for membrane proteins (SLC7A11 has 12 transmembrane domains) and low-abundance transcription factors (NFE2L2/Nrf2). We explicitly state that targeted proteomics (PRM) and PTM-specific proteomics (O-GlcNAc chemoenzymatic labeling) are needed for future validation. All protein-level conclusions (FTH1 ferritinophagy, TFRC iron maintenance, GPX4 inadequate induction) are drawn only for proteins above detection limit.

Q10. 跨平台/跨物种比较中，batch effect如何处理？
Response: We do not merge datasets across platforms or species — each dataset is analyzed independently, and only direction of change (log2FC sign) is compared cross-platform, not absolute effect sizes. For cross-species comparison, mouse gene symbols are mapped to human orthologs (case-insensitive matching). For GSE57338 cross-platform validation (RNA-seq to microarray), we use direction-of-change agreement assessed by binomial test, which is robust to differences in measurement scale between platforms. This conservative approach avoids spurious findings from inappropriate data merging.

Q11. 为什么选择6h再灌注时间点而不是更早或更晚的时间点？
Response: The 6h time point is the most widely used endpoint in MIRI transcriptomic studies, corresponding to the peak of acute inflammatory and cell death responses. It represents a balance between capturing acute transcriptional responses and allowing sufficient time for protein-level changes. The GSE193997 time-series (0.5–12h) and GSE307385 (0–24h) provide temporal context: the peak expression of Nrf2 targets (Hmox1 17.4-fold, Slc7a11 5.9-fold) occurs at 6h, coinciding with the lowest Ogt expression (2.1-fold decrease from Sham at 3h). We acknowledge that different time points may reveal different aspects of the regulatory axis.

Q12. 本文的主要局限性是什么？
Response: We acknowledge nine specific limitations in the Discussion: (1) proteomic coverage is limited and ~50% mRNA-protein concordance indicates dominant post-transcriptional regulation; (2) all data are from public repositories with inherent protocol heterogeneity; (3) snRNA-seq is from rat, not mouse; (4) scRNA-seq was limited to literature-level cross-referencing; (5) CCC and drug repositioning are computational predictions; (6) n=3 sample sizes constrain statistical power; (7) the regulatory model rests on a multi-step inference chain; (8) OGT protein abundance and O-GlcNAc modification states were not directly measured; and (9) this is a hypothesis-generating computational study — no wet-lab validation was performed. We provide six specific testable hypotheses with experimental designs (Discussion Para 5).

Q13. 为什么没有提交到更高的期刊（如Cell Metabolism）？
Response: Cell Reports is the ideal venue for this work because: (1) the journal's emphasis on multi-omics resource papers and transparent reporting of both positive and negative results aligns with our study design; (2) the 7-figure format allows us to present the complete discovery arc (from DEG to cell-type dichotomy to paracrine signaling to external validation to disease-stage specificity); (3) Cell Reports' STAR Methods format ensures full computational reproducibility; and (4) the companion paper (computational modeling + drug repurposing) is targeted to a more specialized venue (Cell Systems/Bioinformatics), providing a natural two-paper publication strategy.
